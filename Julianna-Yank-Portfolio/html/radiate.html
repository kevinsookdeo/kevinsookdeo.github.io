INTRO

Radiate

Welcome to the first application that aims to bring music lovers together! Radiate has introduced new "virtual events" in order to cater to COVID. Join the community to meet new friends, find parties, rides, squad groups and much more!

 

Design Opportunity

When I first began my internship, Radiate already had most of their application built out. I worked closely with the CEO and the Project Manager to continously establish branding and brainstorm new social media poster ideas to help expand their social awareness. I also helped them add new and tweak user interfaces that they had.

 

The Problem

The CEO realized that all of the lesbian dating apps out there did not accurately represent the LGBTQ community and does not satiate all of their needs.

"How can we continue to design for the Radiate brand and increase the number of users on the application?"

 

Welcome to the Radiate Brand!

Radiate already had their logo and colors determined when I started interning for them. The colors are representative of the beautiful colors within a music festival and the logo is almost shaped like a circle, which represents unity.

Let the Design Process Begin!

At the time I began working with the Radiate team, they already had a good establishment of their UI, so my job was basically to help them edit certain screens and create posters with them. Since it was my first time doing this type of work, the CEO already had a layout they loved for promoting previous events, so the process of me making the posters was simple: follow the original template, with updated festival screens!

RESULTS

Key Results from the Changes

  • App has over 6,600 ratings on the App Store
  • App has 4.8/5 stars on both the Apple and Google Play Stores!
  • Received over $600k in funding from investors!
  • Increased downloads and brand awareness by over 35%!
 

What Have I Learned?

  • How to use Sketch and Hootsuite social media manager app!
  • To ALWAYS double check final design edits before handing them off to your project manager!
 

Final Words

This was an amazing experience for me because this was the first time I was ever introduced to UX/UI Design! I have this internship to thank for introducing me to something that I enjoy! I loved working with the team, and the first time that I saw their app on the App Store, I immediately also fell in love with it and wanted to work for them!
