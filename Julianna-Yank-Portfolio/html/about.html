Love at First Sketch

Growing up I have always had art as a huge influence in my life thanks to my mother. She grew up teaching photography as a part of her life, and so therefore I would say art has always been one of my favorite subjects at aschool. Since I was a young girl, I was always a lover of technology, so in college, I thought I wanted to go for something like computer science. I quickly realized that computer science was not the route I wanted to take because of all the math required. I started to feel hopeless in figuring out what I wanted to do in life. Little did I know, Hunter College offered web design and graphic media courses under the New Media major track. Finally, I found something that satiated both my love for art and technology!

Fast forward a few years, I landed the first internship related to my major, at a company called Radiate. For those who do not know this wonderful application, Radiate connects people who attend music festivals together! While working for this company, I was introduced to UX/UI design and IMMEDIATELY fell in love!

I loved everything about the design process! Starting with user research, all the way to the hand off stage everything about it sparked my interest! I remember the first time using Sketch to redesign and edit certain mobile screens and the excitement that flooded my body. I was thrilled that I discovered a career that I was truly passionate about!

In a way, I have radiate as a company to thank for helping me realize my true passion. Since then, I have been working for other companies in order to satisfy my desire for creativity and technology. I strive to continue to enhance my skills on a daily basis, and will continue to search for exciting opportunities!
